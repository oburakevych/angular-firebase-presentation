Does every app
require a backend?

  • Most of the apps do simple data transfers and persistence of state
  • Most mobile apps have all business logic on the client side
  • With HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript MVC business logic moves to the frontend
  • Backends play less significant role

Apps that will benefit most
from using BaaS

  • CRUD style apps - perfect candidates
  • Collaborative and realtime apps: games, chats, editors, radio
  • Parts of enterprise apps - big opportunities
  • Apps that don't have a backend, but whant to enhance UX

Backend as a Service shifts the role of app developers, it can enable them to concentrate on innovating
